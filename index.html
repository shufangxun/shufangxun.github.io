Fangxun Shu

Fangxun Shu

I am currently working at Alibaba, collaborating with Prof. Si Liu, Hongsheng Li, and Cihang Xie. I had fun research experiences at Bytedance AI Lab (2020) and Megvii (2019). I obtained the master degree at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (2021) and bachelor degree at Nanjing University of Posts and Telecommunications (2017).

My research primarily focuses on efficient multimodal language models (MLLMs), exploring innovative architectures such as Mixture of Experts (MoE) to enhance model scalability and efficiency, as well as advanced training paradigms such as knowledge distillation to optimize performance while reducing computational costs. Ultimately, I aim to create powerful yet resource-efficient models that push the boundaries of multimodal intelligence.
